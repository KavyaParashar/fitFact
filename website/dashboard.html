What we do

In this new normal, with the advent of digital progression, keeping up with physical activities have become more important than ever before. Keeping in mind ‘social distancing’, people are unable to reach gyms, making self-motivation even more critical. The Fit Fact provides a platform to fulfill this need for motivation. Using this web application, the data from fitness bands can be given as input to the application in order to provide personalized analysis and prediction in the form of eye-catching visualizations. Based on the health information extracted, a person’s mood can be categorically predicted. Moreover, other health parameters can be time series forecasted based on existing pattern.
